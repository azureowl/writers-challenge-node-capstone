    Word Seed Manual

    Welcome, writers! Here's a bit of help to get started with Word Seed!

    • Profile - To view your account information, click on the person icon next to the logout button at the top right. You can edit your profile name, password, and set your writing word count goal.
    • Editor - Just start writing! Word Seed uses Quill.js editor which includes basic formatting tools. Upon login, if a notebook is not selected, you can still write in the editor. The automatic saving feature will create a generically-named notebook for you.
    • Notebook - To view your notebook collection, click on "Notebook" in the navigation menu. Notebooks can be renamed by clicking on the "pencil" icon or deleted by clicking on the "trash" icon.
    • Add Notebook - By clicking on "+ Notebook", you'll toggle a title field into which you can enter the title of your notebook. Hit 'ESC' to cancel or 'ENTER' to proceed.
    • Saving - Your work is automatically saved!
    • Word Tools - Word Seed includes helpful dictionary and thesaurus tools to lookup words, synonyms, and antonyms.
    • Progress - View your progress by clicking on My Progress in the navigation menu. The word count goal can be set in your profile. The word count total is calculated across all your notebooks. To update any changes, just click Refresh.
    • Inspirational Seed - This button will generate a writing prompt. At this time, Word Seed generates light-hearted writing prompts but there is a plan to add serious prompt options in the future.
